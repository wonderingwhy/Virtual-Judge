Welcome to NBU Virtual Judge


Virtual Judge heuristically develops from traditional Online Judge.

It is non-profit as the most of Online Judges, desiring to provide an optimal programing platform for students.

Taking full advantages of the open Internet, Virtual Judge collects the resources from other well-known Online Judges in the world, which makes users to create personalized contests and have more interested in programing.
